HERITAGE CONSERVATION

The restoration of the city’s heritage to its original character, by using archival construction techniques and traditional building materials. A total of 10 historical gates, including Satta Pol, Chand Pol, Amba Pol, Ram Pole, Udia Pol, Delhi Gate, Hathi Pol, Kishan Pol, Brahm.

Pol, and Suraj Pol, were meticulously conserved and restored using the handsome budget which is over 40 Cr.

INTEGRATED INFRASTRUCTURE FOR ABD (WALLED CITY) AREA

An impactful step towards including the 3.4sq. km walled city of Udaipur into the smart city initiative, without disturbing it’s heritage value in order to transform with the modern Infrastructure and upgrade utility support systems.

WATER TREATMENT PLANT

An extensive network of new water distribution system provides 24×7 clean metered water to the ABD area, promoting public hygiene and well-being in thecommunity which would cost the project INR 24 Cr.

SEWERAGE TREATMENT PLANT

Three STPs with capacities of 25 MLD, 10 MLD, and 5MLD were constructed across the city to achieve 100% sewage treatment. The plants are fully automatic with provision of mechanical screens, motorized gates and valves, grit separators, bio-reactors disinfection; centrifuge PLC, SCADA.
