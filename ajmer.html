Ajmer
The Heart Of Rajasthan




Ajmer is one of four cities in Rajasthan that have been identified as part of the Smart Cities Mission. The Public Health Engineering Department is working on the Ajmer Smart City scheme to reorganize and renew the city's water distribution system and infrastructure.



Smart city Projects in Ajmer

Sewerage project

The Ajmer Smart City Ltd (ASCL) has approved Rs 94 crore for a project to stop dumping sewerage water and drainage into Anasagar.




Water distribution system

The Public Health Engineering Department is working on reorganizing and renewing the water distribution system and infrastructure of Ajmer city.




Affordable housing and employment opportunities

The Smart City Challenge Stage 2: Smart City Proposal Ajmer aims to provide affordable housing and employment opportunities.




Cleaning and conserving Anasagar Lake

The major drains coming into the lake are being modified and treated to divert the waste water to the STP at Pushkar Tiraha.




LED screens

The Ajmer Smart City Limited is installing LED screens.
